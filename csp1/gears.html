Sony Alpha a77

My first APS-C camera, I selected it from its Canon counter-part: Canon EOS 7d considering the rich features of Sony's SLT technology. Although there is a huge debate for Electronic vs. Optical viewfinder, it is a matter of balancing the analog and digital features of a camera. And for that, I found the benefits of Sony's SLT tech over traditional SLRs substantially gives me an edge as a hobbyist photographer.
